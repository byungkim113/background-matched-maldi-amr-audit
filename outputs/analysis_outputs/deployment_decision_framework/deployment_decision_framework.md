# Deployment Decision Framework

Action table for interpreting raw AUC, background-matched AUC, support, and calibration together.

## Rules

| scenario                                                     | decision_category                            | interpretation                                                                                         | recommended_action                                                                                           |
| ------------------------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Raw high + background-centered high + acceptable calibration | candidate_for_controlled_deployment          | Signal survives observable co-resistance background and probabilities are not obviously miscalibrated. | Proceed only with local calibration check, locked thresholds, and ongoing drift monitoring.                  |
| Raw high + background-centered high + poor calibration       | ranking_only_recalibrate_before_clinical_use | Ranking may be useful, but probability thresholds are not ready for clinical decisions.                | Use only as a ranking signal until calibration, thresholds, and clinical operating points are locally reset. |
| Raw high + background-centered low                           | background_dependent_retrain_locally         | Raw performance likely depends on local resistant-population or co-resistance background.              | Do not deploy as portable focal-drug model; validate locally and retrain or recalibrate on current ecology.  |
| Raw high + underpowered matching                             | insufficient_matched_evidence                | The audit cannot distinguish focal signal from background with the available matched strata.           | Collect more matched support or additional external labels before making a deployment claim.                 |
| Raw low + background-centered low                            | not_deployment_ready                         | Neither ordinary external performance nor background-controlled performance supports deployment.       | Do not deploy; use for research triage or redesign the model/evaluation target.                              |
