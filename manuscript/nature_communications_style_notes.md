# Nature Communications Style Notes Used For This Draft

Sources checked on 2026-04-29:

- Nature Communications Article page: https://www.nature.com/ncomms/submit/article
- Nature Communications How to submit page: https://www.nature.com/ncomms/submit/how-to-submit
- Nature Portfolio reporting standards and availability policies: https://www.nature.com/nature-portfolio/editorial-policies/reporting-standards

Working constraints applied:

- Article title is under 15 words.
- Abstract is under 200 words and contains no citations.
- Main text follows Introduction, Results, Discussion and Methods.
- Results and Methods use short topical subheadings.
- The figure/table plan stays under 10 display items.
- Main manuscript uses numerical references embedded in the `.tex` file, matching Nature Communications revision guidance.
- Data availability and code availability statements are included.
- Figures are clean vector PDFs generated from source CSVs, not decorative raster mockups.
- Source data for figures are the committed CSV outputs in `outputs/final_framework_outputs/` and `outputs/analysis_outputs/`.

Important note:

Nature Communications is flexible for first submissions, but this package is structured so it can also be adapted for a revision-stage `.tex` submission.
